WHAT WE DO
Wedding Photography

We are the team of creative Wedding Photographers with a passion for candid wedding photography making us more popular as one of the best wedding photographers in Bhiwani, India.

Pre Wedding Photography

We are highly recommended to our client for Pre wedding Photography . It's a best way to create chemistry between the wedding couple and also to allow a photographer to know about likes and dislikes, best postures and best poses of couples.

Cinematography

Wedding cinematography is the art of documenting a wedding by cinematic style. Our team is creative and expert in the proper use of lighting, various camera angles with different lenses, equipment for smooth shooting and solid editing.

School Photography

Relive the nostalgia of school days with our expertly crafted school photography sessions laughter, and innocence, transforming fleeting moments into timeless keepsakes.

Birthday Parties

Make your birthday celebrations unforgettable with our photography services, preserving every smile, hug, and joyous moment as timeless treasures to cherish for years to come.

Baby Photoshoots

Capture the innocence and purity of your little one with our specialized baby photoshoots, where every coo, giggle, and tiny gesture is immortalized in stunning imagery, creating cherished memories for a lifetime.

About Us

Radhe Color Lab, based in Bhiwani, specializes in professional photography services. With 12 years of expertise, we focus on weddings events, pre-wedding shoots, school photography, portfolio shoots and much more. Our commitment is to authentically capture cherished moments while preserving their originality. Beyond being vendors, we see ourselves as artists dedicated to creating enduring memories for you and your family. Explore the narratives we present, and envision your unique story becoming the next cherished chapter.
